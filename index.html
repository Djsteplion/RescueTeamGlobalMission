ABOUT US

The Steward In Charge, in person of Pastor Ademola Stephen; is a man who received the call of God, fourteen(14) years after been saved in september 1984. He was baptized in the Holy Ghost in 1986.

His Service life was under a respectful father in the land untill July 2013, when the Lord comissioned him into a new assignment for humanity through an uncontrollable burden for God's project: "RESCUE TEAM MISSION".

Rescue Team Global Mission is an assignment to the welfare of the village missionaries, widows, widowers, orphans and the physically challenged.

The ministry was established and inaugurated on the 8th day of march 2014, witnessed by some fathers of faith in the land and colleagues in the work of the ministry and several children of God.

Shortly after the inauguration, the team members being five in number, launched to the field every weekend of the month. Within April to December, the team was able to move around 8 villages for the distribution of relief materials to the missionaries and the villagers; training them on 3 different skills for free to empower them for a better life and improve their economic status.

Evangelism is one of our major arms, which has led many villagers to Jesus Christ,and they have also joined the local church in the village.From January 2015 to December 2021, the Team has engaged in several welfare outreaches for the less privilege;

Free medical service. Distribution of Food and clothing materials to widows, widowers and orphans every quarter of the year. Payment of tution fee and school examination fee for orphans. Giving soft grants to the widows, and missionaries' wives in order to start the free skill training they have acquired.

Finally in December 2021, the Lord instructed the steward in charge to God to several villages, gather the missionaries and their church workers and speak the word of comfort and encouragement to them.
